IU and Park Bo Gum to star in new Netflix series 'When Life Gives You Tangerines'

Published January 30, 2024

IU and Park Bo Gum are teaming up again for a new show!

According to Netflix, the South Korean stars will star in the upcoming series "When Life Gives You Tangerines."

It will be helmed by "My Mister" director Kim Won Suk.

IU and Park Bo Gum recently worked together for the show "Thank You for Your Hard Work."

Park Bo Gum is popular for his roles in "Reply 1988," "Record of Youth," "Encounter," and "Love in the Moonlight."

IU, meanwhile, is a top Korean singer and has starred in several K-dramas, including "Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo," "Hotel del Luna," and "Dream High." She is also behind popular songs like “Lilac,” “Bbibbi,” “Blueming,” and “Strawberry Moon."

She also featured in "People Pt. 2," a solo song by BTS member SUGA, also known as Agust D.
