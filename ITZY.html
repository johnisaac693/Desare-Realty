ITZY is returning to Manila for a concert in August

Published January 26, 2024

ITZY is coming back to Manila this year!

According to an announcement, the K-pop girl group will hold a concert at the SM Mall of Asia Arena on August 3. The show will be part of their Born to Be world tour.

ITZY will kick off their tour in February with two concerts in Seoul, South Korea, before heading to the US, Europe, and Asia.

The girls visited the Philippines in January 2023 for a concert as part of their Checkmate world tour.
